3. Data Collection on Our Website

How do we collect your data?

Your data is collected when you provide it to us. This could be data you enter into a registration form or when adding a contact or task. Part of the data is collected to ensure the error-free provision of the website. Other data can be used to analyze your user behavior. In this "Join" project, the data you enter is used to provide the project management functions (tasks, contacts).
